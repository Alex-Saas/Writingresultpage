Dear Sarah, I hope this letter finds you well. I am writing to let you know about an exciting new swimming class that has recently started at our local community centre. The classes are held every Tuesday and Thursday evening, from 6:00 to 7:30 PM. The instructor, Mr. Ahmed, is highly experienced and has been teaching swimming for over ten years. The monthly fee is quite reasonable at just 50 dollars, which includes all the necessary equipment such as towels and swimming caps. I remember you mentioned that you have always wanted to learn how to swim. I think this would be an excellent opportunity for both of us. We could attend the classes together and even practise at the weekends. Please let me know what you think. I would love for us to join together. Warm regards, Alex
