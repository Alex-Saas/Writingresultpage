01. Brand Overview
Brand Summary

Brief description of the product and what it does.

Example:
GoIELTS is an AI-powered IELTS preparation platform that helps learners simulate real exam conditions, practice their skills, and receive intelligent feedback.

Example:
Empower learners worldwide to confidently achieve their target IELTS scores through intelligent and accessible training tools.

Maintain clear space

Don't:

Add shadows or outlines
